# Deal Health Rules

## Red

Any of the following:

- Open Opportunity has a close date in the past.
- Opportunity is in Business Case or Negotiation with an At Risk or failed POC.
- The same measurable success criterion failed more than once.
- A High competitor threat is present without a documented response.
- CRM records contain a material contradiction that affects technical validation or decision readiness.

## Amber

Any of the following without a Red condition:

- No recent SE activity within 21 days for an active technical deal.
- Missing confirmed economic buyer, champion, or technical evaluator.
- Medium competitor threat or repeated competitor mentions.
- POC is active but criteria, owner, or next action are incomplete.

## Green

- No Red or Amber condition is present.
- Technical validation is supported by measurable evidence.
- Stakeholders and next actions are clear.

## Contradiction examples

- POC status is At Risk or Failed while check-in notes say the environment is stable.
- Opportunity stage advanced while technical success criteria remain unmet.
- Competitor threat increases while the account plan still labels the threat low.
- Close date passed while the stage remains open.
