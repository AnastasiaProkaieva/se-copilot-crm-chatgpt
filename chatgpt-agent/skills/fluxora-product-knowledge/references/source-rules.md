# Source and Freshness Rules

## Preferred sources

1. Current approved product, architecture, security, compliance, legal, or pricing document
2. Current approved product one-pager
3. Current approved competitor battlecard
4. Approved master RFP answer
5. Historical deal-specific answer, used only as a lead

## Required metadata for the knowledge library

- title
- owner
- content_type
- product_line
- approval_status
- effective_date
- review_date
- confidentiality
- supersedes

## Conflict handling

When sources disagree:

1. Prefer explicit approval and newer effective date.
2. Do not synthesize a third unsupported answer.
3. Present the conflict and identify the content owner.
